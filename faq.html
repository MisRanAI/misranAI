Welcome to MisranAI

Welcome to MisranAI. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your data. This page explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you engage with our services.

1.Overview

At MisranAI, we design AI-powered solutions with a strong focus on user trust and data responsibility. This policy provides an overview of what data we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to keep it secure.

2. Data We Collect

We only collect the minimum data necessary to provide and improve our services. This may include: Basic contact information (such as name, email, or phone number) when you reach out to us. Business/project details you voluntarily share with us. Technical information (such as browser type, device, or usage patterns) to improve our website performance. We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for profit.

3. How We Use Data

We use the collected information to: Deliver our AI solutions and services effectively. Improve user experience and optimize our website. Provide customer support and respond to inquiries. Share updates, resources, or proposals (only when relevant to you).

4. How We Disclose Data.

We respect your privacy. Your information will only be disclosed when: Required by law or legal processes. Necessary to deliver services in collaboration with trusted partners (under strict confidentiality agreements). You give explicit consent for us to share data. We do not disclose personal data for advertising purposes without consent.

5. Security

Protecting your data is a top priority. We implement strong technical and organizational measures to: Safeguard against unauthorized access. Prevent misuse, loss, or alteration of information. Ensure secure storage and encrypted communication when possible.

6. Advertising.

MisranAI does not run intrusive ads or sell your data for advertising purposes. If we share case studies or success stories, we always seek permission before including identifiable details. Any promotional material we share is purely to inform and educate â€” not to exploit your data.
